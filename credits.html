BIV is a digial interactive novel written with Inform 7.

Click "Play-In Browzer" to play

Code and charcter responses were partially generated by GPT o3-mini. Cover image was generated with DALLE 2.

The project is exported with Vorpole

Plato Babbles is an interactive tracery project.

Click on the screen to show tracery grammar generated sentences.

Backgorund image sourced from:Serrano, E. (2020, July 02). Statue of Plato. World History Encyclopedia.

Text fragments sourced from: Plato. (2021, September 11). The republic by plato. Project Gutenberg.

Obtained via BeautifulSoup, and processed with nltk into a tracery grammar with the aid of DeepSeek V3. Implemented with p5.js and tracery.js

Hand Me Particles is an interactive artpiece that uses hand capture.

Allow camera usage. Then hold up your hand until you see a semi-transparent orange circle. Tap your index finger and thumb to interact.

This project uses ml5.js, p5.js, as well as the help of DeepSeek V3
